Are you curious about the impact of your spent euros on your ad performance?

    How does it work?

    Select the campaign you want to use for predictions. Select the measurement value you want to predict using the spent euros. Select the amount of euros you wish to use.
    The prediction machine will check your data for faulty data schema. If it has a valid schema, it wil continue building your model.
    The validator will check if your model is reliabe and you can export the results to pdf.
